# Enterprise Maturity Certification

Phase 57J requires Phase 57A–57I PASS under one release identity, current signed resilience evidence, all critical controls PASS, no open critical exception, no expired risk acceptance and an overall maturity score of at least 90.

The certificate and evidence manifest are signed with Cosign. Any artifact change invalidates the manifest hash and signature. Certificate validity is 30 days unless invalidated earlier by a governed change.
