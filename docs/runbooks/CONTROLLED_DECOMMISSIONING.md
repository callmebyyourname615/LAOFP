# Runbook — Controlled Decommissioning and Data Exit

Create a plan with effective time, rollback reference, blocking tasks, data-exit requirement, and Operations/Risk/Business approvals. Before execution verify no unsettled transactions, open disputes, liquidity holds, pending settlement, active certificates, routes, webhooks, or regulatory obligations remain.

Data-exit artifacts must be encrypted, hashed, access-controlled, and retention dated. Execute with an independent actor, verify traffic rejection and monitoring cleanup, preserve evidence, then keep the rollback window open until Business sign-off.
