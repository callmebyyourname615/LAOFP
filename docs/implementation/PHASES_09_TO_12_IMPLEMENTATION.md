# Phases 09–12 implementation

This delivery splits the roadmap into ten reviewable sub-phases: 09A harness, 09B target scenarios, 09C settlement/soak/capacity evidence, 10A SSRF policy, 10B outbound allowlist/redirect controls, 10C mTLS trusted-ingress boundary, 10D PII/audit/DAST/key-rotation readiness, 11A DR orchestration, 11B failure/reconciliation evidence, and 12A go-live evidence gate.

Automated tooling does not replace external penetration testing, regulator approval, business approval, or a real DR exercise. Those remain mandatory human gates.
